# Bgee becomes an ELIXIR Core Data Resource

We are happy to announce that Bgee has been selected as an [ELIXIR Core Data Resource](https://elixir-europe.org/platforms/data/core-data-resources) by [ELIXIR](https://elixir-europe.org/). It means that ELIXIR experts consider Bgee as a resource of fundamental importance to the wider life-science community and the long-term preservation of biological data. We are very pleased that our contribution is recognized in data curation and standardization, ontology development, and in creating new knowledge from gene expression data. Bgee joins the list of [38 ELIXIR Core Data Resources](https://elixir-europe.org/platforms/data/core-data-resources).

This is of course great news for us, but more importantly should allow us in the future to better support our activities, in order to continue providing effective access to reusable gene expression data, and develop new tools to support your research activities.
